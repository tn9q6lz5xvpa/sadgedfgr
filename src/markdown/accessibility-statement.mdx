# Accessibility Statement for The Book Haven

**Last Updated:** 31-10-2025

The Book Haven is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all our customers, including those with disabilities. We strive to continually improve the user experience of our website and apply relevant accessibility standards to ensure everyone can enjoy our content and services.

## 1. Our Commitment

We are dedicated to providing an accessible and inclusive experience for all visitors, regardless of their abilities or technology. We actively work to maintain and improve the accessibility of our website and adhere to standards set by:

- **Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG)**: We aim to conform to WCAG 2.1 Level AA guidelines, which help ensure our website is accessible to people with various disabilities.

## 2. Accessibility Features

To make our website accessible, we have implemented the following features:

- **Keyboard Navigation**: Our website can be navigated using a keyboard, allowing users to access menus, links, and forms without a mouse.
- **Text Alternatives**: We provide alternative text for images to ensure users who rely on screen readers can understand the visual content.
- **Adjustable Text Size**: Users can adjust the text size in their browser settings to improve readability.
- **Readable Fonts**: We use clear and legible fonts to enhance the reading experience.
- **Color Contrast**: We ensure that text and background color combinations have sufficient contrast to be easily readable.

## 3. Ongoing Efforts

The Book Haven is committed to ongoing accessibility improvements. We regularly test and review our website to identify and resolve accessibility issues. Our team receives training and stays informed about the latest accessibility standards and best practices.

## 4. Third-Party Content

While we strive to make all content accessible, some third-party content or links on our website may not fully meet accessibility standards. We encourage third-party providers to comply with accessibility standards and are happy to assist users with any challenges they encounter.

Our website undergoes periodic accessibility audits and user testing to ensure compliance with accessibility guidelines. We also utilize automated tools and manual testing to identify and address potential issues.

We are continually working to improve the accessibility of our website. As technology and standards evolve, we will make necessary updates to ensure that our website remains accessible and user-friendly for everyone.
