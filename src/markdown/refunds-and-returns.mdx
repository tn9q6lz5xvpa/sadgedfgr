# Refunds & Returns Policy for The Book Haven

**Last Updated:** 31-10-2025

At The Book Haven, we strive to deliver the highest quality baked goods and ensure our customers are completely satisfied with their purchases. If you are not satisfied, please review our Refunds & Returns Policy below.

## 1. General Policy

Due to the perishable nature of our products, we have a specific policy for returns and refunds:

- **Perishable Goods**: All good books are non-returnable and non-refundable, except in cases of product defects or damage during transit.
- **Non-Perishable Goods**: Items such as gift cards or merchandise may be eligible for returns or refunds under certain conditions.

## 2. Defective or Damaged Items

If you receive a defective or damaged product, we are happy to offer a replacement or refund. Please follow these steps:

1. **Contact Us**: Email us at **[contact@thebookhaven.khoai.com]** within **48 hours** of receiving your order.
2. **Provide Evidence**: Include your order number and photos of the defective or damaged item(s) in your email.
3. **Resolution**: We will review your case and, if eligible, provide a replacement or issue a refund.

_Note_: We may require you to return the damaged or defective product in some cases.

## 3. Returns for Non-Perishable Items

For non-perishable items, such as merchandise, we accept returns under the following conditions:

- **Return Window**: Returns must be initiated within **14 days** of receiving your order.
- **Condition**: The item must be unused, in its original packaging, and accompanied by a receipt or proof of purchase.
- **Return Shipping**: You are responsible for the cost of return shipping unless the item was defective or damaged.

### How to Initiate a Return

1. **Contact Us**: Email us at **[contact@thebookhaven.khoai.com]** with your order details and reason for the return.
2. **Receive Instructions**: We will provide instructions on how to return your item.
3. **Processing**: Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

## 4. Refunds

If your return or refund request is approved, we will process the refund as follows:

- **Payment Method**: Refunds will be credited back to the original payment method used for the purchase.
- **Processing Time**: Please allow **5-10 business days** for the refund to reflect in your account.

## 5. Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven’t received your refund after the processing time, please:

1. Check your bank account or credit card statement again.
2. Contact your bank or credit card company, as processing times may vary.
3. If you’ve done all of the above and still have not received your refund, please contact us at **[contact@thebookhaven.khoai.com]**.

## 6. Exchanges

We do not offer exchanges. However, for non-perishable merchandise, you may request an exchange under the same conditions as our return policy.

## 7. Gift Cards

Gift cards are non-refundable and cannot be redeemed for cash, except as required by law. Gift cards can be used for purchases on our website.
